Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2026

Introduction

Tribal Productions Radio ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Tribal Productions Radio does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information from users. The app operates as follows:

  • No Personal Data Collection: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other personally identifiable information.
  • No Location Tracking: We do not track or access your device's location.
  • No Analytics: We do not use analytics services to track your usage of the app.
  • No Advertising: We do not display advertisements or use advertising networks.

Media Streaming

The app streams video and audio content from our servers. When you stream content:

  • Your device connects directly to our content delivery servers
  • Standard server logs may record IP addresses for technical purposes (security, performance monitoring)
  • No user-specific data is collected or stored

Data Storage

The app may temporarily cache media content on your device to improve playback performance. This cached data:

  • Is stored locally on your device only
  • Is not transmitted to our servers
  • Can be cleared by deleting the app

Third-Party Services

We do not integrate any third-party services, SDKs, or analytics tools that would collect user data.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The app is designed to be safe for all ages.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal information, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. You maintain full control over the app by simply uninstalling it from your device.
